# Strathmore Retail (Online Retail Business): SQL, Power BI

Strathmore Retail is a mid-sized online retail business that sells a wide range of consumer products including lifestyle, home, and everyday essentials. Operating in a competitive e-commerce market, customers can browse products, place orders, and make repeat purchases through the platform. Over time, the business has accumulated transactional data across multiple areas, including customer profiles, orders, and product sales.

Despite steady growth, the company faces several key challenges:

- Identifying and retaining high-value customers  
- Understanding which products contribute most to revenue  
- Improving customer lifetime value and repeat purchase rates  
- Detecting trends in purchasing behaviour over time
